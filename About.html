CLEAN YOUR CAFETIERE EASILY

Wouldn’t cafetiere coffee be great without the mess? Scoof® is a dedicated utensil designed to clean cafetiere's. It has two blades that scoop and clean out the used coffee grounds in a simple twist. The grounds can be tapped in the food bin or used as compost.

Pour off excess liquid

Lift out grounds

Insert Scoof® and twist

Tap into bin

QUALITY COFFEE

Cafetieres make high quality coffee from beans or freshly ground. In a survey the quality of cafetiere coffee was preferred or rated as highly as all other methods apart from expensive bean to cup machines.

LOW COST COFFEE

Cafetiere coffee is less than half the price of coffee pods. A typical pack of fresh coffee will be enough for 25 cups – just 12p a cup.

COFFEE YOUR WAY

More and more of us are choosing our own beans and our own blend, to get the most out of our coffee experience. Cafetiere coffee puts you in charge – your coffee to your taste.
